# Progression of the models
---

## Prototype 1
I had a vision of laptop-like deck. I modeled the main frame and wanted to model hinges with top lid with screen. 

But there was an issue. For some reason even after Klipper software flash & update the printer still was layer shifting... So after some repairing of the 3D printer I gave up.

So I modeled Prototype 2

## Prototype 2
For mk2 I had in mind a different approach. I did not want to have custom laptop anymore. I decided to model ALL-IN-ONE. Screen, compute unit and battery in one enclosure. It will be one big brick.

So I designed main frame for screen. But I still need to figure out how to mount the rasppi and powerbank... I will maybe add a little plastic blocks for the powerbank so it's still plug and play. But the pi is going to be needed to be mounted via bolts. I'll figure it out. 

Also the lid is going to be bolted down with M2.5 bolts. The holes are gonna be drilled sice I do not know how and where to make those holes.
